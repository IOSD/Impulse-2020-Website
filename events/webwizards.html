WEB WIZARDS

Do you think you are creative? Time to put it to test! Participate in our web development event and develop and design a true work piece by digging deep into your imagination and converting it into a real and fascinating wonder!

Venue : Maharaja Agrasen Institute Of Technology

Date and Time : 12-13 May

Registration Deadline : 8 May 11:59 PM

Rules

Design an innovative UI/UX along with frontend for given theme using HTML, CSS and JavaScript or any frontend framework of your liking.

1. Participants must be at the venue on time. Latecomers will not be given any extra time.

2. Internet access will be provided at the venue.

3. The theme will be the same for all teams

4. The teams will get exactly 3 hours to complete their website.

5. Use of frontend frameworks is allowed..

6. Use of pre-made templates is strictly prohibited. Defaulters will be eliminated.

7. The prototypes will be judged on the following parameters:

- Completion of workflow

- Ease of usage

- Design style and colour schem

- Responsiveness

8. Participants must upload their designs to GitHub and submit the link for the repository. Any commits made after the end of the competition will be rejected.

FAQ's

Are there any predefined themes?

No, there are no predefined themes. The theme will be given on the spot to all the contestants.

Do the participants need to bring their own laptops?

The event will be held in the college laboratory; we'll be providing you with a place to work on. However, one can bring his/her own laptop as well. We strongly recommend everyone to bring their own laptops for this event.

How long will the event go on for?

The event may last for 3 hours.

Is it necessary to have a designer in the team?

No. It is not necessary but we encourage you to have a designer on board
